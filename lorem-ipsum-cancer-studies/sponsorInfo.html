Our Sponsors

We would like to thank these sponsors for their support:

Department of Pathology, University of Lorem Ipsum School of Medicine

The research vision of the School of Medicine is to increase the impact of research and discovery on human health, not only in this region but around the world. The School of Medicine works closely in partnership with the University of Lorem Ipsum Medical Center to provide research-intensive, academic and clinically based care for more than .2 million patients each year.

Lorem Ipsum Cancer Institute, Lorem Ipsum Institute of Health

The Loem Ipsum Cancer Institute (LICI) is the nation's leader in cancer research. It is the subdivision of the Lorem Ipsum Institute of Health (LIIH) responsible for researching diagnosis, pathology, detection, and treatment of cancer.
The LIIH is staffed by over 20,000 employees and is where national level medical research is conducted. Located in Bethesda, Maryland, the LIIH was created in 1887 to provide for the medical care of the American public.
